Build Your Future at Murray TAFE

Welcome to Murray TAFE, the region’s premier hub for hands-on, industry-recognised training. Situated in the heart of the Murray River region, our campuses offer modern facilities designed to simulate real-world work environments. Whether you are finishing high school, looking to switch careers, or aiming to upskill for a promotion, we provide the practical education you need to succeed. Our close ties with local employers ensure that what you learn in the classroom directly translates to the skills businesses are looking for today.


Industry-Driven Courses for Local Careers

We offer a diverse range of certificates, diplomas, and short courses tailored to the economic needs of our community. Students can gain hands-on experience in thriving local sectors such as agriculture, health and community services, hospitality, and traditional trades. With flexible learning options, including part-time and online study, we make it easier than ever to balance your education with work and family commitments. Many of our programs are also fully funded under government initiatives, meaning you might be eligible to study tuition-free.


A Supportive Community Dedicated to Your Success

At Murray TAFE, you are never just a number. Our experienced instructors bring years of industry knowledge into the classroom, offering personalised mentorship every step of the way. Beyond academics, our dedicated Skills and Jobs Centre provides free career counselling, resume workshops, and interview preparation to help you confidently transition into the workforce. From enrollment to graduation, we are fully committed to helping you unlock your potential and build a rewarding career right here in our region.


Acknowlegement of Country

We respect and honour Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Elders past, present and future. We acknowlege the stories, traditions and living cultures of Aboriginal and Torres Strait islander peoples on this land and commit to building a brighter future together.
